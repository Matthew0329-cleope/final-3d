The Forest Neurotech Story

2023

Foundation

Forest Neurotech was founded in 2023 by Will Biederman, Tyson Aflalo, and Sumner L Norman with the aim of developing minimally invasive ultrasonic whole-brain computer interface devices.

2025

NHS Trials

A £6.5 million NHS trial is set to begin in March 2025 to investigate the safety and tolerability of their ultrasound brain implant for treating neurological conditions.

About Forest Neurotech

Forest Neurotech is a pioneering neurotechnology company focused on creating next-generation brain-computer interfaces using ultrasound technology. Their aim is to provide precise and less invasive monitoring and treatment for various neurological and psychiatric conditions.

Leadership & Vision

Sumner L Norman

Co-founder & CEO

“Forest Neurotech is pioneering ultrasound brain-computer interfaces to transform the treatment and understanding of brain disorders.”

Learn More

The leadership at Forest Neurotech, including co-founder and CEO Sumner L Norman, envisions a future where brain disorders can be treated with precise and minimally invasive ultrasound-based therapies. Their focus is on developing technologies that can monitor and modulate brain activity at a neural circuit level, offering new hope for conditions like depression, addiction, and OCD.

Driving the Future

Forest Neurotech's core innovation lies in its application of focused ultrasound for brain imaging and modulation. Their Forest 1 device and developing implantable BCI aim to provide a less invasive alternative to traditional methods, offering the potential for personalized therapies for a wide range of neuropsychiatric disorders by precisely targeting neural circuits.
